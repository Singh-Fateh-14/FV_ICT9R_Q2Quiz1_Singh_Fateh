Crypto Description
Dogecoin Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency created by software engineers Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer. Dogecoin is a payment system created as a joke, making fun of the wild speculation in cryptocurrencies at the time. It is considered both the first meme coin, and more specifically the first dog coin. Its initial release was in 2013.
Bitcoin Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency designed to act as money and a form of payment outside the control of any person, group, or entity. This removes the need for trusted third-party involvement in financial transactions.
Shiba Inu Coin Shiba Inu token is a cryptocurrency created in 2020 by an anonymous person or group. It is inspired by the Shiba Inu, a Japanese dog breed, which also serves as the mascot for Dogecoin, another cryptocurrency with meme origins.
